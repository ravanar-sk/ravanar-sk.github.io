Turkey History

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Turkey History ("Application") for mobile devices, created by K.R. Saravana Kumar.

Offline Core Principle: The Application is designed to work completely offline. We do not host, run, or integrate any backend database servers, and your historical data (favorites, reading progress, quiz scores) remains strictly local on your device.

1. Personal Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, physical address, phone number, or precise location). All historical exploration, favorite entries, quiz history, and settings preferences are stored locally on your device using a secure SQLite database.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising

The Application integrates Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain non-personal device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, prevent fraud, and perform analytics.

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this Application and/or other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page.

3. Data Retention and Safety

Since all data generated by the Application (such as bookmarked entries, quiz progress, and badges) is saved exclusively to your device's local storage, deleting the Application will permanently erase this data. We have no way to restore it as it is never synced with any remote cloud server.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal info, please contact us immediately, and we will take necessary steps to remove it.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
