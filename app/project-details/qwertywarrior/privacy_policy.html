Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QWERTY Warrior (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use our mobile game on iOS and Android.

1. Information We Collect

QWERTY Warrior does not require users to create an account. The app may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, such as score, difficulty level, streak, progress, settings, and game preferences.

If leaderboard features are enabled, your score, display name, and related leaderboard information may be submitted to platform leaderboard services such as Apple Game Center or Google Play Games, depending on your device and account settings.

2. Advertising

The app may show advertisements, including rewarded ads that allow you to continue gameplay after a game over. Ads may be provided by third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob.

These advertising providers may collect or use device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, and other information as described in their own privacy policies. We do not control how third-party advertising networks collect or process data.

3. Analytics and Performance

We may use analytics or crash-reporting tools to understand app performance, fix bugs, and improve the gameplay experience. These tools may collect basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and usage events.

4. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Run and improve the game experience
  • Save local progress and preferences
  • Display scores and leaderboard rankings
  • Show ads, including rewarded ads
  • Analyze crashes, bugs, and app performance

5. Third-Party Services

QWERTY Warrior may use third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Games Services
  • Apple Game Center
  • Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics, if enabled

These services are governed by their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they handle your information.

6. Children’s Privacy

QWERTY Warrior is a general audience typing game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Storage and Security

Gameplay preferences and progress may be stored locally on your device. We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with third-party services only as needed for ads, analytics, crash reporting, leaderboard functionality, platform services, legal compliance, or app operation.

9. Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking or personalized ads through your device settings. You may also manage leaderboard and gaming profile settings through Apple Game Center or Google Play Games.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
