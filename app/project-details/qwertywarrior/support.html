About the Game

Keyboard Warrior challenges you to type displayed words as fast as possible using a custom on-screen QWERTY keyboard before the countdown timer ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

A word will appear on the screen. Type the word using the on-screen keyboard before the timer reaches zero. Correct letters are highlighted, and incorrect letters are shown immediately.

What are the difficulty levels?

  • Easy: 3-4 letter words with a 10-second timer
  • Medium: 5-7 letter words with a 7-second timer
  • Hard: 8-12 letter words with a 5-second timer

How does scoring work?

Points are awarded based on the word length. Your streak increases when you complete words correctly without failing.

Can I pause the game?

Yes. Use the pause button during gameplay to pause and resume the game. You can also end the current game from the pause menu.

How does Continue with Rewarded Ad work?

After game over, you may be offered the option to watch a rewarded ad to continue. If completed, the game resumes with the same word and the timer reset.

Why are ads not loading?

Ads may not load if there is no internet connection, ad inventory is unavailable, or the ad provider is temporarily unable to serve ads. Please check your connection and try again later.

Why is my leaderboard score not showing?

Leaderboards may require you to be signed in to Apple Game Center or Google Play Games. Please check your device account settings and ensure you have an active internet connection.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app if the game screen does not respond.
  • Check your internet connection for ads and leaderboard features.
  • Update the app to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Restart your device if performance issues continue.
