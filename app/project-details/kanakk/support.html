Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Kanakk?

Pick a game mode, choose your difficulty, and start solving math questions before the timer runs out. Each correct answer earns a point. The faster and more accurate you are, the higher you climb.

What are the game modes?

Time Attack: 60 seconds, answer as many as you can.
Endless: No round timer, but three wrong answers ends the game.
Survival: Three lives, with a timer that gets faster as your streak grows.

How do I turn off music or sound effects?

Tap the gear icon on the home screen to open Settings. You can toggle background music and sound effects independently and adjust their volume.

Why is the leaderboard empty?

Kanakk uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center on your device. Scores submit automatically after every game.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. The core gameplay works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed to submit scores to Game Center and load occasional ads.

How do I remove ads?

Kanakk shows at most one full-screen ad per day, and only on the Game Over screen. Banner ads appear on the home and game over screens. Watching the optional rewarded ad in Endless mode lets you continue your run with the same score.

Is Kanakk safe for kids?

Kanakk is designed to be kid-friendly. There's no chat, no social features, no in-app purchases, and no personal data is collected. Ads served by Google AdMob follow standard family-friendly content guidelines.
