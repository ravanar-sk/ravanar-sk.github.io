Privacy Policy

How Kanakk handles your data

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Overview

Kanakk ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information our mobile app collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Kanakk is a math game for iOS and Android. It does not require an account, does not collect personally identifiable information directly, and works offline for all core gameplay.

Information We Collect

Information We Do Not Collect

Kanakk does not ask you to create an account, sign up, or provide any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or address. We do not collect or store this kind of information on our own servers.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores small amounts of data on your device only, using your device's local storage. This includes:

  • Your audio settings (music and sound effects on or off, volume levels)
  • The date the last full-screen ad was shown (used to enforce the once-per-day ad limit)

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

Kanakk integrates with the following third-party services. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads and prevent fraud.

Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple Game Center (iOS only)

If you sign in to Apple Game Center on your iOS device, your scores are submitted to Game Center leaderboards. Game Center is operated by Apple. Your Game Center identity (display name and player ID) is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Learn more in Apple's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Games (Android only)

On Android, leaderboards are powered by Google Play Games Services. If you choose to sign in, your gamer profile and scores are managed by Google.

Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.

How Information Is Used

Information collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Show ads (Google AdMob)
  • Display global leaderboard rankings (Game Center / Play Games)
  • Improve the app and detect technical issues

We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with third parties beyond the integrations described above.

Your Choices

Opt Out of Personalized Ads

You can limit ad tracking on your device:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → toggle off Personalized Ads.
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → reset advertising ID or opt out of personalization.

Sign Out of Leaderboards

You can sign out of Apple Game Center or Google Play Games from your device's system settings at any time. Your scores will no longer be submitted.

Delete Local Data

Uninstalling the app removes all data Kanakk has stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

Kanakk is suitable for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not have user accounts, chat features, or social interaction. Ads shown through Google AdMob follow Google's family-friendly content guidelines.

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us so we can address it.

Data Security

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no centralized server holding your data. Data stored on your device is protected by the security mechanisms of your operating system. Information handled by third-party services follows the security practices of those providers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification or app store update notes.
