Privacy Policy

App: Developer Options Shortcut
Effective date: April 2, 2026

Developer Options Shortcut is a simple Android app that helps users open the Android Developer Options settings screen quickly, like a shortcut.

In normal use, this app does not collect, store, share, or transmit personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Developer Options Shortcut does not collect personal information, account information, location data, contacts, photos, files, messages, or any other user content.

The app only opens the Developer Options settings page available on your Android device.

2. How the App Works

When you open the app, it redirects you to the Android system Developer Options screen or the relevant system settings page supported by your device.

Any actions taken on that settings screen are handled entirely by the Android operating system and your device manufacturer, not by this app.

3. Data Storage

This app does not use remote servers and does not store personal data for tracking or profiling purposes. Its function is limited to opening a system settings page.

4. Permissions

Developer Options Shortcut is designed to work with minimal or no special permissions, depending on your Android version and device implementation.

The app does not request access to sensitive information such as your camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or location unless explicitly stated in the app listing or future updates.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote backend services as part of its core functionality, unless separately disclosed in the app store listing or future updates.

6. Sharing of Information

Since Developer Options Shortcut does not collect your personal information, it does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

7. Children’s Privacy

Because this app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children or other users.

8. Security

Since the app does not collect or transmit user data, there is no user data processed by the app that requires online storage or transfer security measures.

Your device settings and Developer Options remain subject to the protections and controls provided by Android and your device manufacturer.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the app’s functionality changes. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page.
