Privacy Policy

App: Language Selector
Effective date: April 1, 2026

Language Selector is a simple Android app that only opens the Android system language selector settings page on your device. The app does not provide user accounts, cloud services, messaging, analytics, advertising, or data collection features.

In normal use, Language Selector does not collect, store, share, or transmit your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Language Selector does not collect personal information, usage data, location data, contacts, photos, files, or any other user data. The app simply opens the relevant Android system settings screen so you can manage your device language preferences directly through Android.

2. How the App Works

When you use Language Selector, the app redirects you to the Android system language settings page available on your device. Any language changes you make are handled entirely by the Android operating system, not by this app.

3. Data Storage

Language Selector does not store personal data on our servers or in the app for tracking purposes. Because the app only opens a system settings page, it generally does not need to retain user information.

4. Permissions

Language Selector is designed to work with minimal or no special permissions, depending on your Android version and implementation. It does not request access to sensitive data such as your contacts, microphone, camera, location, or storage unless explicitly stated in the app listing.

5. Third-Party Services

Language Selector does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or remote servers as part of its core functionality, unless separately disclosed in the app store listing or future updates.

6. Sharing of Information

Since Language Selector does not collect your personal information, it does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

7. Children’s Privacy

Because Language Selector does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

8. Security

Since the app does not collect or transmit user data, there is no user data processed by the app that requires online storage or transfer security measures. However, your device and Android system settings remain subject to the security protections provided by your device manufacturer and Android.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the app’s functionality changes. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page.
