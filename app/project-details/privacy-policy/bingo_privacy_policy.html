Privacy Policy

Bingo Offline Multiplayer

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bingo Offline Multiplayer handles information when you use the app. Your privacy is important to us. The app is designed primarily for offline play and does not require internet access for core gameplay.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect minimal to no personal data.

2. Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location data

3. Non-Personal Information

The app may store limited information locally on your device, such as:

  • Player name entered by you
  • Game progress
  • Settings and preferences, such as sound or music

This information is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

4. Offline Multiplayer and Local Connectivity

The app supports offline multiplayer gameplay using local connectivity methods such as local network or peer-to-peer communication where available.

  • No personal data is transmitted over the internet for gameplay
  • Only gameplay-related information, such as game state or player actions, may be exchanged locally between devices
  • No account creation or login is required

5. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only. We do not use cloud storage, remote databases, or online account syncing for the core app experience.

6. Third-Party Services

Bingo Offline Multiplayer does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or tracking technologies to collect personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

Since we do not collect personal data or transmit it to remote servers, privacy risk is minimal. Game-related data remains on your device.

9. Your Choices

You may manage your local app data at any time:

  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
