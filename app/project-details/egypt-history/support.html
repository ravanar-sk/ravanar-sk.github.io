Thank you for using Egyptian History! We are dedicated to providing you with the best educational, offline-first experience. Below you will find answers to frequently asked questions and information on how to get in touch with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
A: No. Egyptian History is fully functional offline-first. All biographies, timelines, quizzes, monuments data, achievements, and images are packaged and bundled directly inside the application, meaning you can learn on the go anywhere in the world without using mobile data.
Q: Are my quiz scores or reading achievements synced to a cloud profile?
A: No. To safeguard your absolute privacy, all reading completions, quiz results, favorites, and unlocked badges are stored locally in a secure SQLite database on your device. We do not transmit or upload this data to any servers. If you delete or reinstall the app, this local progress will reset.
Q: How do I unlock achievements and badges?
A: The app tracks your reading progress as you scroll. When you read 95% of a figure, event, or monument page, it is marked as complete. Reading multiple profiles unlocks specific badges (e.g. "Scribe", "Archaeologist", or "Historian"). You can also unlock the "High Priest" badge by scoring a perfect 5/5 on a quiz session! You can check your progress inside your user Profile tab.
Q: Why are ads shown in the app?
A: Subtle ads displayed at the bottom of the screen help fund ongoing research, application maintenance, and development so that we can keep this comprehensive guide free for everyone. We strive to keep these ads unobtrusive and respect your reading experience.
