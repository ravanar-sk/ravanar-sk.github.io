Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Egyptian History, an educational mobile application built for iOS and Android. Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy details how we handle user data.

1. Information Collection and Use

Egyptian History is an offline-first application. We believe in absolute user privacy. Consequently:

  • We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store any personal information (such as names, emails, IP addresses, or location data) on any remote servers.
  • All of your application progress—including read states, bookmarks/favorites, quiz history scores, and unlocked badges—is stored exclusively and securely on your local device in an offline SQLite database.

2. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

To support the maintenance and free availability of the application, we display subtle banner advertisements provided by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) at the bottom of the screens.

Google AdMob may collect and process device identifiers, ad tags, or other anonymous data to serve relevant ads (including personalized or non-personalized advertisements) in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

You can adjust your ad personalization preferences directly within your device's native settings (Google Settings on Android, or App Tracking Transparency prompts on iOS).

3. Permissions

The application only requests standard network permissions required by the Google Mobile Ads SDK to fetch banner ad creatives. The app does not request or require access to sensitive device features such as your camera, microphone, contacts, storage files, or precise GPS location.

4. Children's Privacy

Egyptian History is built as an educational resource suitable for students and history enthusiasts of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly request or gather data from children under the age of 13. If you have any inquiries, please contact us.
